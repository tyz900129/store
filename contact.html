FAQ

Common questions.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — we ship to 42 countries. Standard shipping is 5-8 business days and is free on orders over $80. Express options are available at checkout.

What's your return policy?

We offer 30-day returns on all unused items. If something doesn't work out, email us and we'll send a prepaid return label.

How do I care for wool products?

Spot clean with cold water and a mild detergent. Air dry flat — never put wool in the dryer. For deeper cleaning, dry clean or hand wash in cool water.

Do you offer wholesale?

Yes. We partner with select boutiques and pet stores. Email wholesale@pawpatrol.shop with your shop info and we'll be in touch.

Can I cancel or change my order?

You can cancel or change your order within 2 hours of placing it. After that, we may have already started preparing your shipment.
