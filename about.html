★ OUR STORY

We make pet goods the way grandma used to.

PawPatrol began in 2026 in a one-room studio in Brooklyn. We were tired of plastic pet products that look like they came from the same factory — so we started making our own. Today a small team of craftspeople hand-finishes every piece we sell, using natural materials and small-batch production.

OUR VALUES

Three things we won't compromise on.

SUSTAINABILITY

Made to last. Built to be loved.

We've designed every product for a 10-year lifetime. The wool is biodegradable, the leather is vegetable-tanned, the ceramic is dishwasher-safe. When something wears out, send it back and we'll repair or replace it — forever, no questions asked.

Our packaging is 100% recycled and recyclable. Our studio runs on renewable energy. And 1% of every order goes to local animal shelters.
